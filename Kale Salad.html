Blue Cheese Kale Salad

This salad has it all: fresh kale, sweet apples, creamy blue cheese, and a simple tangy dressing that pulls everything together. Itâ€™s super easy to make (like, 10 minutes tops) and hits that sweet spot between light and satisfying. Great for lunch and a quick way to get your veggies and fiber in!


Nutrition Facts

  • ~250 calories

  • ~22g carbs

  • ~8-10g protein

  • ~14g fat

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped kale

  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese

  • 1/4 honey-crisp apple

  • Dijon Mustard

  • Olive oil

  • Apple Cider Vinegar

  • Pepper and Garlic Powder

Steps to Make

  • Wash kale in cold water and massage for 3-5 minutes to reduce bitterness.

  • Add 1/4 cup blue cheese crumbles on top

  • Cut and dice 1/4 honey-crisp apple

  • In a separate bowl add 1 tbsp Dijon Mustard and 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar and mix well

  • Add a dash of pepper and garlic powder to your dressing as well as 1/4 tsp olive oil and 1 tbsp water

  • Mix throughly


    • Image of dressing

  • Add dressing on top and enjoy!
