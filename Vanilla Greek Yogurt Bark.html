Vanilla Greek Yogurt Bark




Cool off this summer with a simple frozen snack that’s just as good as it looks—this vanilla Greek yogurt bark is topped with fresh fruit and a little crunch. It's creamy, sweet, and perfect for warm days,a quick protein boost between meals, or a sweet treat after dinner!




Nutritional Facts

  • ~25 calories per piece

  • ~3.5g of protein per piece

Ingredients Needed:

  • Vanilla Greek Yogurt

  • Berries (Strawberries/Blueberries/Raspberries)

  • Honey

  • Granola





How to Make:

  • Spread a 1/4 inch thick layer of Greek Yogurt onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper

  • Wash and cut raspberries and strawberries in half and place in the yogurt flat side down

  • Top with a drizzle of honey and a sprinkle of Granola




    • Picture of Bark Pre-Freeze ^^^

  • Freeze Bark 15-20 mins
  • Break into pieces and enjoy!!
